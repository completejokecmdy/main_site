Complete Joke FAQs

We're on the first Sunday of every month (8-10pm) at the Micklegate Social, York. Doors open at ~7:45pm.

The nearest parking available is at Nunnery Lane , York

You can sign up for a spot here (forms open 2 weeks before the show if it says form closed) All spots are 5 mins.

We understand that it's disappointing to not get a spot. At the same time we have so many people apply for a limited number of spots that we sadly can't have everyone on that we'd like to. It's worth messaging us at our instagram page about three days before the show as this is when we confirm with acts whether they can still make it and we sometimes have dropouts.

Please try checking your message requests folder and spam on your email. Unfortunately, some email filters can be overly jealous. If you're on the line up we will let you know a week before the show itself.

Tagging us @complete_joke_comedy on insta would be amazing because we want the show to grow and hopefully a scene to grow in York (On that note you should should also check out dodgy comedy, another great mic in York)

The show is PWYW (pay what you want) and the money is divided equally between acts. When you sign up it would be great to use an email associated with PayPal as this makes payments a lot easier. As a performer you also get the following discounts on drinks at the venue: £3 for any ICF Pint (Social Pint, Hells Pint), £3.50 singles and £5 Double of house spirits.
